Gaurav Bhalerao is a postdoctoral researcher in the Nuffield Department of Clinical Neurosciences working in neuroimaging. He is affiliated with the Oxford University Centre for Integrative Neuroimaging and serves on the steering committee of the OxCIN Global Scholars programme. A certified Carpentries instructor and Software Sustainability Institute Fellow, Gaurav is committed to building reproducible workflows, championing FAIR data, and helping researchers across disciplines gain practical computational skills.
