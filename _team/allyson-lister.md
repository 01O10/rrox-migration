Allyson Lister leads on projects related to [FAIRsharing](https://fairsharing.org/), a curated educational resource on data and metadata standards inter-related to repositories and data policies across all disciplines. She is the FAIRsharing Content and Community Lead within the Data Readiness Group at the Oxford e-Research Centre. Allyson works closely with researchers, funders, and publishers to champion FAIR data principles and to make it easier for teams to choose the right standards for their work.
