Here are links to resources relating to open and reproducible research in and around Oxford. Feel free to [get in touch]({{ '/get-involved/' | relative_url }}) to suggest additions.

- [Catalogue of Bias](https://catalogofbias.org/) — a collaborative project mapping the biases that affect health evidence and beyond.  
- [Codebar Oxford](https://codebar.io/) — free programming workshops aimed at making the tech community more diverse.  
- [Data Readiness and FAIRness](https://datareadiness.eng.ox.ac.uk/) — a research group based at the Department of Engineering Science working on data transparency, reproducibility, and scholarly publishing. The group develops and maintains [FAIRsharing](https://fairsharing.org/), an educational resource on data and metadata standards (for sharing and reporting) inter-related to repositories, knowledge bases, and data policies.  
- [EQUATOR Network](https://www.equator-network.org/) — an international initiative based in Oxford that seeks to improve the reliability and value of published health research by promoting transparent reporting and the wider use of robust guidelines.  
- [IT Learning Centre](https://skills.it.ox.ac.uk/) — offers a wide range of [Digital Skills courses](https://skills.it.ox.ac.uk/whats-on#/) for staff and students.  
- [NC3Rs](https://www.nc3rs.org.uk/) — the UK’s National Centre for the Replacement, Refinement and Reduction of Animals in Research, with an Oxford hub providing courses and [resources](https://www.nc3rs.org.uk/experimental-design) on robust experimental design.  
- [Open Access Oxford](http://openaccess.ox.ac.uk/) — publishes guidance on open access at Oxford, including [Oxford Open Scholarship case studies](http://openaccess.ox.ac.uk/2019/07/22/open-scholarship-at-oxford-case-studies/).  
- The [Oxford local group of the Royal Statistical Society](https://www.ndorms.ox.ac.uk/csm/royal-statistical-society-oxford-local-group) — runs regular talks and lectures on statistical topics that are open to academics and non-academics alike.  
- [Oxford Research Software Engineering (OxRSE)](https://www.rse.ox.ac.uk/) — provides professional software support for research projects as well as free training and bookable software surgeries.  
- [Research Data Oxford](http://researchdata.ox.ac.uk/) — the University’s official policy hub for research data management.  
- [Research Support](https://researchsupport.admin.ox.ac.uk/) — outlines [principles for the responsible use of research metrics](https://researchsupport.admin.ox.ac.uk/information/principles) in line with Oxford’s support for [DORA](https://sfdora.org/), and provides a [Research Integrity: Introductory Core Course](https://researchsupport.admin.ox.ac.uk/support/training/ethics#collapse409401) for all disciplines.  
- [UKRN Open Research Primers](https://www.ukrn.org/primers/) — brief, practical introductions to making research more transparent, robust, and accessible.
