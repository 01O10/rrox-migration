RROx is committed to making its web content accessible to as many people as possible. If you have trouble accessing any material on this site, please email [rrox-steering@maillist.ox.ac.uk](mailto:rrox-steering@maillist.ox.ac.uk) so we can provide the information in an alternative format and improve future content.

The site follows the [University of Oxford accessibility guidance](https://edu.admin.ox.ac.uk/accessibility) and aims to meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Let us know if you spot anything that does not meet these expectations.
